Frequently asked questions

Everything you need before booking — tap a question to see the answer.

How far is it from IMPACT Muang Thong Thani, and how do I get there?
Only about 7-10 minutes (approx. 3.8 km). We can arrange a motorbike taxi or car to the venue entrance — faster than searching for parking at the hall.
Is the hotel pet friendly? Any conditions or fees?
Yes — we have pet-friendly rooms with a spacious garden nearby. A small pet fee of 300 THB per pet applies.
How much are the rooms and what's included?
Standard rooms from 1,090 THB/night, Family rooms 1,890 THB/night. All include breakfast, Wi-Fi, AC, TV and hot water. No hidden fees.
What are the check-in and check-out times?
Check-in from 14:00, check-out by 12:00. Staff are available 24 hours, so late arrivals after concerts or events are welcome.
Is parking available?
Yes — spacious free parking right at the hotel, no need to circle for a spot.
Are there places to eat nearby?
Breakfast is included, and there are convenience stores and tasty local restaurants close by. Ask us for recommendations.
Is the hotel safe? Is anyone on duty at night?
Yes — staff are on duty 24 hours and there are CCTV cameras throughout the property, so you can sleep easy.
How do I book and pay? Can I reschedule or cancel?
Booking is easy via LINE or by phone at 094-962-5955, 24 hours a day. You can change your stay dates for free if you let us know at least 2 days in advance — we understand events can move and plans can change.
